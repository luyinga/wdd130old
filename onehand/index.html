Welcom to One Hand

One Hand is an organisation who is helping people who are drug addicted by giving them advice and helping them stop using drugs. One hand is also helping people who are staying in the street by giving them clothes, food and anything they are in need, we are also doing door to door to collect food, clothes, jackets, blankets and anything that can help people so that we can give to the people who are in the street. We are there to give advice to those people who are in the street and those who are using drugs so that they can get out of the situation they are in and serve the community.
